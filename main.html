Manali


Manali is a beautiful hill station located in Himachal Pradesh, India. It is famous for its scenic mountains, snow-covered peaks, adventure sports, and peaceful environment, making it a perfect holiday destination.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Rohtang Pass – A breathtaking snow-covered mountain pass.
  2. Solang Valley – Popular for adventure activities like paragliding.
  3. Hadimba Temple – A historic temple surrounded by cedar forests.
  4. Old Manali – Known for its cafes and relaxed vibe.
  5. Manu Temple – A sacred place with mythological importance.

Must-Try Foods

  • Siddu – A traditional Himachali bread.
  • Trout Fish – Fresh river fish cooked with local spices.
  • Thukpa – A warm noodle soup perfect for cold weather.
  • Momos – Steamed dumplings filled with vegetables or meat.
  • Babru – A Himachali version of kachori.
