Manali


Manali is a beautiful hill station located in Himachal Pradesh, known for its scenic beauty and adventure activities.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Rohtang Pass – Snow-covered mountains.
  2. Solang Valley – Adventure sports hub.
  3. Hadimba Temple – Historic temple.
  4. Old Manali – Cafes and chill vibe.
  5. Manu Temple – धार्मिक place.

Must-Try Foods

  • Siddu – Traditional dish.
  • Trout Fish – Famous local fish.
  • Thukpa – Noodle soup.
  • Momos – Dumplings.
  • Babru – Himachali snack.
